During the 2022 T20 World Cup, Kohli scored 82* and won the match for his team , creating history by winning a match almost impossible to win. His six against Haris Rauf is considered as one of the greatest sixes of all time.He rated this as his best innings in the format due to the magnitude of the game [105][106] Wisden deemed this particular innings as the acme of the tournament .[107] Kohli finished as the highest run-scorer in the tournament, with 296 runs to his name at an average of 98.66
